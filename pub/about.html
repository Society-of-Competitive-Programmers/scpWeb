Purpose and Description
Purpose

      The Society of Competitive Programmers is dedicated to encouraging students to participate in programming competitions and hackathons. We facilitate the formation and mentoring of teams by providing technical workshops and hands-on programming sessions.

Types of Events

      There are four different types of regular meetings in our organization in order to both encourage and prepare our members for programming competitions and hackathons. The Types of Meetings are:

  • Technical Workshop
  • Expo Meeting
  • Algorithmic Programming Session
  • Technical Programming Session

      With these events, you will be able to learn how to apply certain skills to hackathons with our Technical Workshops, work in a large group to prepare for the Engineering Expo in our Expo Meetings, code problems to prepare for programming competitions in our Algorithmic Programming Sessionss, and apply skills, learned or no, in our Technical Programming Sessions. More information about these meetings is described below.

General Information

      The Society of Competitive Programmers is a special interest student organization that was founded in the Spring Semester of 2018 with the intention of encouraging students from the University of South Florida to go to hackathons. More general information about our organization is described below.

Types of Events
Technical Workshop

      The Technical Workshops that we hold in our organization are 2 hour sessions where we teach our members different project that they can program, especially at a hackathon. The topics of these workhshops are all Technical based with the purpose of having our members be capable of replicating the concepts taught in this session.

Expo Meeting

      The Expo Meetings that we hold in our organization are meetings where members come together to work on our project for Engineering Expo in the Spring Semester. In these meetings, members can work together in groups and both learn and apply their skills in order to complete this project.

Algorithmic Programming Session

      The Algorithmic Programming Sessions that we hold in our organization are 2 hour sessions where members complete programming problems. The purpose of this meeting is to allow our members the opportunity to practice general programming, which is generally useful in programming competitions. The problems that are given has varying levels of difficulty, and members are encouraged to work together or ask for help at their leisure. Points are given during the event, and the people who has the most points by the end of the semester gets a prize.

     50pts - Attendance      20pts - Easy      30pts - Medium      70pts - Hard

Technical Programming Session

      The Technical Programming Sessions that we hold in our organization are 2-hour simulations of hackathons. You can even call them Mini-Hackathons! In these sessions, members have the chance to apply their programming skills during the event by trying to fulfill 3 topics that are given during the session, typically based on the previous workshop. Points are given during the event, and the people who has the most points by the end of the semester gets a prize.

     50pts - Attendance      40pts - Completion of any Category      30pts - Best in Category
